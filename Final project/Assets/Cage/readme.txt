This Asset pack contains model of a Metal Cage.
In medieval times, a metal cage was used as a warning to people. A condemned man could be put in a cage like this to die.
By placing the cursor on the door you can open or close it by clicking on it.

Textures are in .PSD(diffuse, specular) and .PNG file format (normal), resolution are 1024 x 1024. 

They are all configured correctly for use with Unity, just import into your project.

Hope you like it!
